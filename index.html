How I work
I find where workflows break before they scale
I naturally identify the gaps between how a team thinks it operates and how it actually does — then I build the systems, content, and processes that close that gap before it becomes a problem.
I prioritize clarity over activity
I do not mistake motion for momentum. Every program, playbook, or initiative I build is tied to a clear outcome — adoption, retention, ramp time, or revenue. If it does not move the needle, it does not get built.
I balance high standards with high empathy
I hold myself and the people I work with to a high bar — but I know that accountability lands better when people feel seen and supported. I bring both, and I know when to lead with which.
I step into the gaps
I am the person colleagues come to when something needs an owner, a thought partner, or someone who will just figure it out. I operate with high agency and low ego — I care about the outcome, not the credit.
